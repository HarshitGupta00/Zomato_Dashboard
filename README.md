# 🍽️ Zomato Business Intelligence Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

This project focuses on building an interactive **Business Intelligence dashboard** to analyze Zomato’s operational data, including **sales performance, user behavior, and city-wise trends**.

The goal was to transform raw transactional data into **actionable insights** that can support business decisions such as market expansion, customer retention, and performance tracking.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

* 📊 **Sales Analysis** – Track total revenue, order volume, and ratings
* 👥 **User Analytics** – Analyze active users, gained vs. lost users, and demographic distribution
* 🌍 **City Performance** – Compare sales across cities and identify high-growth regions
* 📈 **Trend Analysis** – Year-over-Year sales trends and performance changes
* 🎯 **Interactive Filters** – Top N, city-wise, and year-wise filtering for dynamic exploration

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Power BI** – Data visualization and dashboard creation
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Calculated measures and KPIs
* **Excel / CSV Data** – Data preprocessing and storage

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Identified **high-revenue cities** contributing significantly to overall sales
* Detected **low-retention user segments**, highlighting potential churn areas
* Observed **sales trends over time**, helping understand growth patterns
* Compared **user engagement across demographics** for targeted strategies

---

## 💡 Business Impact

* Enabled **data-driven decision-making** for market expansion
* Helped identify **regions requiring customer retention strategies**
* Provided a centralized view of **sales, users, and performance metrics**
